## Usage Notes

<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> workspace Commercial Plan users need to successfully register for **<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Console and <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center accounts** in order to proceed with subsequent usage billing and fee settlement processes.

- [<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Console](https://<<< custom_key.studio_main_site >>>/) account can track the data intake scale of the current workspace, detailed billing information, etc., which will be synchronized to the specified Billing Center account.
- [<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>/) account can be bound via `Workspace ID` to achieve **unified fee management at the workspace level**, and offers multiple payment settlement options for you to choose from.
