# Alibaba Cloud Account Settlement
---

That is, directly recharge and settle accounts through an Alibaba Cloud account. One Alibaba Cloud account supports associating multiple workspaces of multiple sites for cost settlement.


## First Purchase of <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Commercial Plan

<img src="../../img/bill-by-ali.png" width="70%" >

1. [Enter the registration process](../../plans/commercial-register.md);
2. In the third step, when selecting the activation method, choose [Alibaba Cloud Market Activation](#purchase);
3. Enter the workspace name and select the workspace language;
4. Fill in the account ID and product instance ID;
5. Check to agree with the terms;

In this step, the system will create a Billing Center account for you synchronously. Later, you can log into the billing center platform using the username/password used during registration (i.e., the username/password used in the first step of registering the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> account) to view detailed billing information.

## Modify Settlement in the Billing Center Platform

1. Log in to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>>[Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>);
2. Enter Workspace Management;
3. Select the workspace and click the change button on the right;
4. Choose **Alibaba Cloud Account Settlement**;
5. First [go to Alibaba Cloud to activate](#purchase) <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service;
6. After purchase, obtain [Account ID](#id) and [Product Instance ID](#entity-id);
7. Click confirm.

## How to Obtain Product Instance ID {#entity-id}

After confirming the activation application for <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> on Alibaba Cloud Heart Selection, you will be directly redirected to the list of purchased services on Alibaba Cloud. Below the purchased <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service, you can see the **Instance ID** of the product. Copy this **Instance ID** and fill it into the **Product Instance ID** field in the modify settlement method dialog box.

![](../img/10.aliyun_market_5.png)



## Cancel Subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> on Alibaba Cloud {#cancel}

1. In the Alibaba Cloud console, click to enter **Cloud Market > Purchased Services**;
2. In **Purchased Services**, you can view the subscribed <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> instance. Click the **Close** button at the top right corner of that instance;
3. In the pop-up **Business Closure** dialog box, click **Confirm**. You will then see that the instance has been marked as **Closed**, indicating that the subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> has been canceled on Alibaba Cloud.

![](../img/10.aliyun_market_1.png)


![](../img/1.aliyun_cancel_2.png)


![](../img/1.aliyun_cancel_3.png)

**Note**: After canceling the subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service, <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center will unbind all associated workspaces from this account and switch to the settlement method of <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center account. At the same time, you will receive an email notification regarding this change.
