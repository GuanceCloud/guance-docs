# Amazon Cloud Account Settlement
---

That is, directly recharge and settle accounts through an Amazon cloud account. One Amazon cloud account supports associating multiple workspaces, multiple site workspaces for cost settlement.

## First-time registration of <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Commercial Plan {#register}

1. [Enter the registration process](../../plans/commercial-register.md);
2. In the third step, when selecting the activation method, choose [Amazon Cloud Store Activation](#subscribe);
3. Enter the workspace name, select the workspace language;
4. Check the agreement acceptance box;
5. Click to activate.


### Subscribe to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> in the Amazon Cloud Marketplace {#subscribe}

1. Log in and enter the [Amazon Cloud Marketplace](https://awsmarketplace.amazonaws.cn/marketplace/pp/prodview-duyx7ds3f3cq2) <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> product page, click **Continue Subscription**;
2. After successful subscription, click **Set Up Your Account**.

## Changing Settlement on the Billing Platform

1. Log in and enter <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> [Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>);
2. Enter Workspace Management;
3. Select a workspace, click the change button on the right side;
4. Choose **Amazon Cloud Account Settlement**;
5. First [go to Amazon Cloud Subscription](#subscribe) for <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service;
6. After subscription completion, the system will automatically redirect to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center;
7. Select the workspace;
8. Click **Confirm Submission**.

### Binding Multiple Amazon Cloud Accounts for Workspace Settlement

This means binding multiple workspaces under the same Amazon cloud account for settlement.

1. Enter [<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>/#/signin) > Workspace Management;
2. Click **Change Settlement Method**;
3. Under **Registered Cloud Account Settlement**, select the Amazon cloud account you want to bind;
4. Click **Confirm** to successfully bind.

## Canceling <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Subscription in Amazon Cloud {#cancel}

1. Log in and enter Amazon Cloud, in the top-right corner account selection, choose **Your Marketplace Software**;
2. In **Manage Subscriptions**, find the subscribed <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service, click **Manage**;
3. Enter the service management page, click **Actions > Cancel Subscription**;
4. In the pop-up dialog box, click **Yes, Cancel Subscription**.

**Note**: After canceling the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service subscription in Amazon Cloud, all workspaces bound to this account settlement in the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> billing center will have their associated cloud account deleted, and all related workspaces will undergo a [lock](../../billing-center/workspace-management.md#workspace-lock) operation. You can unlock the workspace in **Billing Center > Workspace Management**.
