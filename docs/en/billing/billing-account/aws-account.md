# Amazon Cloud Account Billing
---

This involves directly recharging and settling accounts through an Amazon Cloud account, which supports associating multiple workspaces and multiple site workspaces for billing under a single Amazon Cloud account.

## When Selecting the Commercial Plan for the First Time <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> {#register}

1. [Enter the registration process](../../plans/commercial-register.md);
2. On step three, when selecting the activation method, choose [Amazon Cloud Store Activation](#subscribe);
3. Enter the workspace name and select the workspace language;
4. Check the box to agree to the terms;
5. Click activate.


### Subscribing to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> via the Amazon Cloud Marketplace {#subscribe}

1. Log in and enter the [Amazon Cloud Marketplace](https://awsmarketplace.amazonaws.cn/marketplace/pp/prodview-duyx7ds3f3cq2) <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> product page, and click **Continue Subscription**;
2. After successfully subscribing, click **Set Up Your Account**.

## Changing Billing in the Billing Center Platform

1. Log in and enter the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> [Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>);
2. Go to Workspace Management;
3. Select the workspace and click the change button on the right;
4. Choose **Amazon Cloud Account Billing**;
5. First [go to the Amazon Cloud subscription](#subscribe) for <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> services;
6. After completing the subscription, the system will automatically redirect you to the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center;
7. Select the workspace;
8. Click **Confirm Submission**.

![](../img/10.aws_change_1.png)

![](../img/15.aws_register_10.1.png)


### Binding Multiple Amazon Cloud Accounts for Workspace Billing

This means binding multiple workspaces under the same Amazon Cloud account for billing.

1. Enter the [<<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>/#/signin) > Workspace Management;
2. Click **Change Billing Method**;
3. Under **Registered Cloud Account Billing**, select the Amazon Cloud account you wish to bind;
4. Click **Confirm** to complete the binding.

![](../img/10.aws_change_1.png)

![](../img/10.aws_change_2.png)


## Cancelling Subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> on Amazon Cloud {#cancel}

1. Log in to Amazon Cloud and select **Your Marketplace Software** in the top-right corner;
2. In **Manage Subscriptions**, find the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service you subscribed to and click **Manage**;
3. Enter the service management page and click **Actions > Cancel Subscription**;
4. In the pop-up dialog box, click **Yes, Cancel Subscription**.

**Note**: After cancelling the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service subscription on Amazon Cloud, all workspaces associated with this account in the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Billing Center will have their cloud accounts unlinked and will be [locked](../../billing-center/workspace-management.md#workspace-lock). You can unlock these workspaces in **Billing Center > Workspace Management**.
