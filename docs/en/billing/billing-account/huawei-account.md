# Huawei Cloud Account Settlement
---

That is, directly top up and settle accounts through the Huawei Cloud account. One Huawei Cloud account supports associating multiple workspaces and multiple site workspaces for fee settlement.

## Select when first registering for the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> Commercial Plan

1. [Enter the registration process](../../plans/commercial-register.md);
2. In the third step, when selecting the activation method, choose [Huawei Cloud Market Activation](#market);
3. Input the workspace name, select the workspace language;
4. Enter the account ID;
5. Check the box to agree with the terms;
6. Click activate.

In this step, the system will create a billing center account for you synchronously. Later, you can log into the billing center platform using the username/password used during registration (i.e., the username/password used in the first step of registering the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> account) to view detailed billing information.


### Activate on Huawei Cloud Market {#market}

Log in and enter the [Huawei Cloud Market > <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> SaaS Edition](https://marketplace.huaweicloud.com/contents/8990a122-f456-43a4-892c-a458be31e336#productid=OFFI845592654693900288) product purchase page.

Return to the Huawei Cloud **Purchased Services List** to check the purchased <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service; if there are no purchased products yet, refresh the page.

## Change Settlement on the Billing Center Platform

1. Log in and enter <<< custom_key.brand_name >>>[Billing Center](https://<<< custom_key.boss_domain >>>);
2. Enter workspace management;
3. Select the workspace, click the change button on the right side;
4. Choose **Huawei Cloud Account Settlement**;
5. First [go to Huawei Cloud to activate](#market) <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service;
6. After purchasing, obtain the Huawei Cloud [account ID](#id) and [product instance ID](#instanceid);
7. Click confirm.


<img src="../../img/13.huaweicloud_19.png" width="70%" >


## Cancel Subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> on Huawei Cloud {#cancel}

1. Log in and enter Huawei Cloud Market > Purchased Services;
2. Click the **More > Close Resource** on the right side of the resource;
3. Continue clicking **Close Resource**;
4. Refresh the current page, you can see that the resource status has changed to **Closed**, indicating that the subscription to <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> on Huawei Cloud has been canceled.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_26.png)

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_26.1.png)


**Note**: After canceling the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> service subscription on Huawei Cloud, all workspaces in the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> billing center bound to this account will have their associated cloud account deleted, and the corresponding workspaces will be locked. You can unlock the workspace, and after unlocking, the default settlement method will be the <<< custom_key.brand_name >>> billing center account.


## How to Obtain Huawei Cloud Account ID {#id}

1. Log in to the [Huawei Cloud](https://www.huaweicloud.com) console with your account;
2. Click the account avatar in the upper-right corner;
3. In the dropdown list, click **My Credentials**;
4. On the API Credentials page, you can view the **Account ID**.

<img src="../../img/1.huaweicloud_6.png" width="65%" >


<img src="../../img/1.huaweicloud_8.png" width="65%" >


## How to Obtain Huawei Cloud Instance ID {#instanceid}

On the Huawei Cloud Market, go to **My Cloud Market > Purchased Services**. Enter the product details page, and under the current service's **Application Information**, you can view the Huawei Cloud Instance ID.

![](../img/huaweicloud-0803.png)
