# AI Intelligent Assistant
---

When using the Guance platform, you may encounter technical issues or operational questions. For this purpose, we have developed an AI Intelligent Assistant that uses natural language processing and machine learning technology to quickly understand and answer your questions, helping you efficiently use the platform.

To start a conversation, enter the Guance console and click on the AI Intelligent Assistant icon to open the chat box.

<img src="img/guance-ai.png" width="70%" />

Enter your question, and the AI Intelligent Assistant will do its best to provide an answer:

<img src="img/guance-ai-1.png" width="60%" />

If you are not satisfied with the response, click the **Regenerate** button.

## Generate Charts {#chart}

Through AI automatic analysis of input text data, it can intelligently generate corresponding charts, thus solving the cumbersome nature of manually creating charts, improper chart selection, and complex data visualization issues.

Click the "Generate Chart" option above the text input box to enter the new mode. After posing your question, you can directly click **Generate Chart in One Click**.

<img src="img/guance-ai-2.png" width="60%" />

> For more details, refer to [Chart Settings](../scene/visual-chart/index.md).
