# Huawei Cloud Account Settlement
---

This involves directly topping up and settling accounts through a Huawei Cloud account, where one Huawei Cloud account supports associating multiple workspaces across multiple sites for fee settlement.

## Selecting During the First Registration of Guance Commercial Plan

1. [Enter the registration process](../../plans/commercial-register.md);
2. On the third step, when selecting the activation method, choose [Huawei Cloud Market Activation](#market);
3. Input the workspace name, select the workspace language;
4. Enter the account ID;
5. Check the box to agree to the terms;
6. Click activate.

During this step, the system will synchronize and create a billing center account for you. Later on, you can log into the billing center platform using the username/password provided during the initial registration (i.e., the username/password used in the first step of registering for Guance) to view detailed billing information, etc.

### Activating via Huawei Cloud Market {#market}

Log in and enter the [Huawei Cloud Market > Guance SaaS Edition](https://marketplace.huaweicloud.com/contents/8990a122-f456-43a4-892c-a458be31e336#productid=OFFI845592654693900288) product purchase page.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_1.png)

Click **Activate Now**, it will prompt you with **Purchasing Guance SaaS Edition**. After agreeing to the terms, click **Activate Now**.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_3.png)

It will show **SaaS task submission successful**, click **Return to My Cloud Market**.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_4.png)

Return to the Huawei Cloud **Purchased Services List** to check the purchased Guance service. If there are no purchased items yet, refresh the page.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_6.png)

## Changing Settlement Method on the Billing Center Platform

1. Log in to the Guance [Billing Center](https://boss.guance.com);
2. Enter Workspace Management;
3. Select the workspace, click the change button on the right side;
4. Choose **Huawei Cloud Account Settlement**;
5. First [go to Huawei Cloud to activate](#market) Guance service;
6. After purchasing, obtain the Huawei Cloud [Account ID](#id) and [Product Instance ID](#instanceid);
7. Click confirm.


<img src="../../img/13.huaweicloud_19.png" width="70%" >

## Canceling Subscription to Guance on Huawei Cloud {#cancel}

1. Log in to Huawei Cloud Market > Purchased Services;
2. Click the resource's right-side **More > Close Resource**;
3. Continue clicking **Close Resource**;
4. Refresh the current page, and you should see that the resource is now in a **Closed** state, indicating that the subscription to Guance has been canceled on Huawei Cloud.

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_26.png)

![](../img/13.huaweicloud_26.1.png)

**Note**: After canceling the subscription to the Guance service on Huawei Cloud, all workspaces associated with this account in the Guance Billing Center will delete the linked cloud account and lock the corresponding workspaces. You can unlock the workspaces, and after unlocking, they will default to being settled by the Guance Billing Center account.

## How to Obtain Huawei Cloud Account ID {#id}

1. Log in to the [Huawei Cloud](https://www.huaweicloud.com) console with your account;
2. Click the account avatar in the top-right corner;
3. In the drop-down list, click **My Credentials**;
4. On the API credentials page, you can view the **Account ID**.

<img src="../../img/1.huaweicloud_6.png" width="65%" >

<img src="../../img/1.huaweicloud_8.png" width="65%" >

## How to Obtain Huawei Cloud Instance ID {#instanceid}

In the Huawei Cloud Market, go to **My Cloud Market > Purchased Services**. Enter the product details page, and under the **Application Information** section of the current service, you can view the Huawei Cloud instance ID.

![](../img/huaweicloud-0803.png)
